Why are we doing this?
The problem in e-commerce and the relevance of the project
Nowadays a significant share of trade moves to the Internet, and small shops faces several issues. The most important of them are increasing competition, impossibility of personal communication with client and too wide selection.
One of the most productive ways to solve the problem is to implement recommendation system to website.
But development of such system is overpriced and requires considerable time. Our project allows small online shops to rent recommendation system, save time and money.
